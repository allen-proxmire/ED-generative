## Quick read

ED is a **13-Primitive Generative Substrate Ontology** (Generative Primitives System). The 13 primitives (P01–P13) are *postulated*, not derived. The empirical case is cross-domain reach.

Genre: same as Newton (three laws, posited), Einstein (two postulates, posited), Schrödinger (equation, posited), and the operational-reconstruction tradition (Hardy, CDP, Masanes-Müller, Coecke-Kissinger).
