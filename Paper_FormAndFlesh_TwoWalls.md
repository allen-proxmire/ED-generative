## Abstract

Across five independent substrate-evaluation results, the Event-Density program returns the same shape: **ED generates the *form* of a domain — its skeleton, template, or structural invariant — and withholds the determined *flesh* — the metric field, the continuum law, the escape.** In channel capacity (A1) the structural severance is exact while no intrinsic scalar exists; in charge (B4) the topological skeleton (quantized winding, integral Gauss law) is realized while the Coulomb field is withheld at the discrete layer; in the primes the finite-local *template* (the scale-invariant 1.700-bit structure) is reproduced exactly while the *escape* (primality, the Möbius function) is provably unreachable; in gravity (GR-I/II) the weak-field metric and the classical tests are derived while the gravitational *class* departs from pure General Relativity at one scalar; in the continuum limit the substrate is a kinetic lattice-gas while a diffusion PDE is reachable only by deleting ED's determinism. The synthesis's load-bearing content is that **these 'no's are not interchangeable.** They fall into **two distinct walls**: a **coarse-graining wall** (the determined continuum — field, metric, PDE — as a shadow one layer up; B4, GR, continuum) and a **finite-memory wall** (the escape that requires unbounded memory; primes). The first is mostly *open* — its flesh is reachable by an admissible extension (GR), or only by leaving ED (continuum), or sits one DCGT layer up (charge). The second is *proven* — anchored to an external theorem (the sieve parity barrier), the only proven negative in the program. Mapping each result to its wall and modal status yields a precise reading: ED is a generator of form whose determined-continuum flesh is, in every case, the coarse-grained shadow — but *whether and how* that shadow is reachable varies by domain, and conflating the cases is the error this paper is written to forbid. The framing is monism with two description-scales (ED the fact-level, the continuum its forgetful summary), now an empirical pattern rather than an assertion — with the honest caveat that a recurring form is not a confirmed theory.

## 1. The Recurring Shape

The substrate-evaluation program has now produced enough results to see a pattern that none of them, alone, would establish. In every domain examined, ED supplies the **form** of the structure and withholds the **determined flesh**. The terms are used precisely throughout: **form** = the discrete, local, relational structure ED supplies (a topological invariant, a statistical template, a kinetic equation, an emergent metric, an exact locality); **flesh** = the determined continuum or escape object — the part that requires either *coarse-graining* (a field, a metric, a PDE) or *unbounded memory* (the prime escape) to exist at all. The flesh, where it can be located, is the **coarse-grained shadow** of the form, not a substrate object.

This is not a slogan applied after the fact; it is what five independent build-and-run results returned, each reached on its own terms and most of them by self-correction. The value of stating it as a synthesis is twofold: it makes the recurrence visible (a coincidence across five unrelated domains is not a coincidence), and — more importantly — it lets us see that the *withholdings are not all the same kind*, which is the result that actually advances understanding.

### 1.1 Scope and regime of validity

This synthesis covers **only the five substrate-evaluation results completed to date** — capacity (A1), charge (B4), primes, gravity (GR-I/II), and the continuum limit. It does **not** assert that the two-walls structure holds in all domains, only that it *recurs in these five*; a sixth domain could fit the pattern, refine it, or break it (§7 states the falsifier). The five were not chosen to flatter the thesis: each was selected because it is **falsifiable** (build-and-run, or theorem-anchored), each probes a **different aspect** of the substrate (information, topology, computability, geometry, hydrodynamics), and they are **mutually independent** — no result among them depends on another's holding.

One positioning note. The gravity entries (GR-I/II) reflect the **post-pivot curvature-emergence arc**: the earlier scalar-tensor/MOND gravity papers (Paper_033 and companions) *postulated* an unspecified acoustic metric, which GR-I **realizes explicitly** as the bandwidth metric and GR-II classifies as khronometric. Those earlier papers are *sharpened, not discarded* — their MOND content (`a₀`, BTFR) remains live as the open khronon↔MOND question — but where this synthesis speaks of "gravity," it means the post-pivot results, and readers of the earlier arc should consult GR-I §7 / GR-II §9 for the explicit reconciliation.

## 3. The Load-Bearing Insight: Two Walls

The 'no's look alike — "form yes, flesh no" — but they are not the same wall. They divide cleanly into two, distinguished by *what kind of object the flesh is* and *what kind of limit blocks it*.

**Wall 1 — the coarse-graining wall.** Here the flesh is a **determined continuum object** — a field (charge's Coulomb), a metric (gravity), a PDE (diffusion). The form is the discrete/local structure; the flesh is its coarse-grained limit. This wall is about *emergence and layering*, and it is **mostly open**: nothing proves the continuum object unreachable; the question is always "does ED's coarse-graining supply it?" The continuum-of-points is dischargeable in principle.

**Wall 2 — the finite-memory wall.** Here the flesh is an **uncomputable / infinite-memory object** — the prime escape (primality, the Möbius sign). The form is the finite-local template; the flesh requires unbounded reach. This wall is about *computability*, and it is **proven** — anchored to the sieve parity barrier and Sarnak disjointness, with the √N activation horizon as the mechanism. It is the program's only proven negative, and it owes that status entirely to having an *external* ruler.

**Primes is the only result behind Wall 2; everything else is at Wall 1.** This is the synthesis's central content, because it forbids two tempting and wrong inferences:

- *"Primality is provably unreachable, so the others are too."* No — the others are at a different wall, with no analogous impossibility theorem. There is no parity-barrier for "ED yields Maxwell" or "ED yields the diffusion PDE."
- *"The continuum objects are reachable (e.g. GR's metric was derived), so primality is too."* No — attaining a coarse-grained shadow (Wall 1) says nothing about crossing the memory ceiling (Wall 2). They are different boundaries.

The mathematical root of the non-transfer: **the coarse-graining wall concerns the continuum-of-points infinity; the finite-memory wall concerns the uncomputable / infinite-memory infinity. These are distinct mathematical obstructions — a limit-of-refinement versus a bound-on-computation — and no verdict at one carries any implication at the other.**

The 'no's are heterogeneous. Treating them as one wall is the error this synthesis exists to prevent.

## 4. How the Flesh Is Withheld: a Trichotomy, and Three Infinities

Within Wall 1, *how* the flesh is withheld varies, and the variation is itself informative — a trichotomy of modal status:

- **Reachable by an admissible extension (gravity).** The weak-field metric is *derived*; the field-equation form is Lovelock-forced; the gravitational flesh is largely *supplied* through an admissible extension (the dynamical-bandwidth rule), departing from textbook GR only at the khronon. This is the case where ED reaches *most* of the flesh while staying ED.
- **Reachable only by leaving ED (continuum).** The diffusion PDE appears only when the determinate selection rule is deleted (inject-noise → random walk) and never from pure ED (no emergent mixing). The flesh is reachable, but *crossing to it costs the substrate*.
- **One DCGT layer up, open (charge).** The Coulomb field is the standard continuum limit of the lattice holonomies; the latent Maxwell action is present in the coherence term; whether ED's coarse-graining selects it is well-posed and unproven.

And A1 sits slightly apart: there the withheld "flesh" is a *positive intrinsic scalar*, and it is **provably absent by locality** (capacity exactly zero) — the boundary's content is observational/common-cause, not a transmittable quantity.

This trichotomy maps onto the program's **three infinities**: the **continuum-of-points** (Wall 1 — field, metric, PDE; dischargeable by coarse-graining in principle), the **infinite-memory / uncomputable** (Wall 2 — the prime escape; proven-blocked), and the **continuous-to-continuous** map (e.g. GR → Newton, a limit between two already-continuous theories, which poses neither wall). These three are **distinct mathematical obstructions**, not three sizes of one obstruction — which is the deepest reason the walls do not transfer: the flesh is a different *kind* of object in the primes case than in all the others.

## 5. The Honest Accounting

What the pattern is, and is not:

- **It is a structural property, recurring across five unrelated domains.** A topological invariant, a template invariant, a derived metric, a kinetic equation, an exact locality — all returned the same form-yes / flesh-as-shadow shape, most by self-correction. Five independent domains agreeing is not a coincidence; it reflects something real about the architecture (discrete, local, determinate, irreversible).
- **It is not a confirmation of ED.** The recurrence shows ED *has* a definite reach with a definite structure; it does not show ED is *correct* as physics. "Form yes" is a pattern; it is also an **unpaid bill** — generating a domain's form is not reproducing its confirmed observations, and ED still owes those (the diffusion phenomenon, Maxwell's numbers, the strong-field metric).
- **The 'no's are not interchangeable** (§3) — the single most important guard against over-reading the pattern.
- **The one proven negative is the exception, not the template.** Primes *looks* like the hardest case, but only because it is the one with an external ruler (the parity barrier). The others are *un-disproven*, which is weaker than *attainable*. Absence of an impossibility proof is not a promise.

## 6. The Philosophical Reading

The pattern grounds a specific ontological picture, and grounds it *empirically* rather than by assertion. ED is the **fact-level** — discrete, local, determinate commitments. The determined continuum — field, metric, PDE — is its **forgetful coarse summary**, as thermodynamics is to statistical mechanics. This is **monism with two description-scales, not two layers of reality** — to forestall the natural misreading: **this is not a two-layer ontology; it is one substrate with two descriptive regimes, fact-level and coarse-grained**, exactly as molecules-and-temperature is one reality under two descriptions. The continuum-limit result makes this concrete and falsifiable — the determinate substrate does *not* relax into the continuum's smooth law from within (you reach the PDE only by leaving ED) — and the charge result makes it sharp — the Coulomb field is literally the standard lattice→continuum limit, living one DCGT layer up.

The picture has familiar ancestors, and the synthesis sits deliberately among them rather than apart: the **statistical-mechanics → thermodynamics** relation (the canonical fact-level/summary pair), the **renormalization-group** insight that coarse descriptions can gain symmetry and simplicity the substrate lacks, **lattice gauge theory** (the discrete-holonomy → continuum-field dictionary used in the charge result), and the **computability limits** tradition (the primes wall is a cousin of the classical undecidability boundaries). What the synthesis adds is not a new philosophy of emergence but a *measured instance* of it: one substrate, five domains, the same form/flesh split returned by experiment and theorem rather than argument.

The reading also explains why ED's reach has the *shape* it does. ED's two signature commitments — orientation-blind (determinate) selection and irreversible commitment — are simultaneously what make it a **determinacy engine** (it produces definite facts) and what **foreclose the determined continuum at the substrate layer** (it makes facts, not relaxed averages). The same architecture does both. The form is what a determinacy engine generates; the flesh is what its coarse-graining leaves behind.

## 7. What Would Advance It

The synthesis points to one open question per domain, and they are not the same question:

- **Charge (Wall 1, open):** does ED's DCGT measure select the Maxwell-action configuration as the continuum limit of the holonomies?
- **Gravity (Wall 1, admissible-extension):** the khronon parameters (`α₁, α₂`) and the strong-field field equation — phenomenology, declared in GR-II.
- **Continuum (Wall 1, leaving-ED):** whether a *different* ED instantiation (the posited Q-C/UDM model) is diffusive by construction.
- **Primes (Wall 2, proven):** closed — the wall is a theorem; nothing here is expected to move it.
- **Capacity (A1):** a transfer-entropy / common-cause decomposition formalizing the observational-vs-interventional split.

The pattern's own falsifier: a domain in which ED supplies the determined flesh *directly at the substrate layer* (not as a coarse-grained shadow) would break the form/flesh distinction — and, separately, transferring one wall's verdict to the other (e.g. proving a Wall-1 object unreachable by a parity-barrier-style theorem, or reaching the prime escape with finite memory) would break the two-walls structure.

## 8. Position Statement

**Across five independent substrate-evaluation results, ED generates the form of a domain and withholds the determined flesh, which — where locatable — is the coarse-grained shadow of the form.** The load-bearing refinement is that the withholdings are **heterogeneous**: a **coarse-graining wall** (charge, gravity, continuum — the determined continuum as a shadow one layer up, mostly open) and a **finite-memory wall** (primes — the uncomputable escape, proven, externally anchored). Within the first wall the flesh is reached by an admissible extension (gravity), only by leaving ED (continuum), or sits one DCGT layer up (charge); the second wall is a theorem. The cases do not transfer.

**What this paper claims.** The form/flesh shape is a real, recurring structural property of the ED architecture, and its 'no's are heterogeneous in exactly the two-walls structure described — a reading that organizes the program and forbids the over-transfers between domains.

**What this paper does not claim.** It is a synthesis, not a result; the pattern is a structural property, not a confirmation of ED as physics; "form yes" is an unpaid bill, not paid observations; and no domain's verdict transfers to another. The component papers are authoritative for their own results.

**Series context.** The capstone of the post-Facts substrate-evaluation program: A1 (capacity), B4 (charge), Primes (finite-memory), GR-I/II (gravity), and the continuum-limit result, read as one architecture with two walls.
