# Event Density — A Generative Substrate Ontology for Physics

[![DOI](https://zenodo.org/badge/1237222292.svg)](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20149496)

> The universe is a participation graph of discrete, relational becomings that commit
> irreversibly and sparsely, one at a time. Smooth spacetime, fields, and the constants are
> the coarse-grained pattern that process makes, seen from far enough away — with the arrow
> of time written into the law, not the boundary conditions.

**Event Density (ED) is a substrate-based reading of physics.** The universe is not a smooth
continuum running its equations on top of itself. It is a process of discrete events, and what
standard physics has been describing all along is the coarse-grained pattern those events make.

Picture a large LED billboard. A wave moves across it — but nothing actually crosses the screen.
There is only a grid of discrete lights switching on and off in coordinated ways; the "motion" is
a sequence of state-changes in the grid underneath. **The pattern is real. The thing moving is not.**
ED says the universe is built that way: smooth spacetime is the appearance, and the substrate
underneath is a network of discrete events updating one at a time, each constrained by every
update before it. What we measure as motion, gravity, charge, probability, and time are patterns
in how those events relate.

## The substrate, in one pass

ED bottoms out *below* space, time, field, and particle. The base is a **participation graph** of
discrete becomings; everything familiar is the coarse-grained pattern it makes. The framework is
**thirteen posited primitives (P01–P13) and two memory kernels** — V1 (finite-width retarded) and
V5 (cross-chain finite-memory) — that carry the dynamics.

**The core triad.** A **micro-event** (P01) is a discrete *act of becoming* — no position, no
duration, no trajectory, no persistent identity. It is defined only by its **participation** (P02)
relations to other micro-events — the relational fabric itself, not a force or a field — set on a
discrete index of channels and loci with spatial homogeneity (P03).

**Chains** are the first composite: a `(sequence, rule)` pair. The sequence gives a trajectory; the
**update rule gives identity**. Persistence lives in the rule, not in enduring stuff — an electron
continues because its rule keeps generating new micro-events in the same relational pattern.
Particles, photons, neutrinos are chains.

**The continuum state** is `(ρ, b)`: event density ρ counts the graph's vertices (the namesake
scalar), bandwidth b weights its edges. This pair is what coarse-grains into every smooth field
description physics uses.

**The rest of the thirteen, each load-bearing.** Four-band bandwidth including a *commitment-reserve*
band (P04); polarity-transport, the single causal cone (P05); spatial dimension D = 3+1 (P06,
behind Newton's R² and the holographic 4πR²); channel structure (P07); the substrate scale ℓ_ED,
identified with the Planck length (P08 — the step that closes Newton's G); U(1) polarity (P09);
rule-types, so matter and gauge are *different rules*, not different states (P10); **commitment**
(P11), the irreversible collapse of multi-channel participation to one channel — the arrow; the
stability landscape Σ = Coh − Str − Grad (P12); and **time homogeneity** (P13).

## The load-bearing core

A few structural moves carry the whole program:

- **Commitment (P11) is the determinateness engine.** It does not make determinacy from nothing; it
  *actualizes* relational potential — a definite pattern with an open outcome — into irreversible
  fact, the substrate-level analogue of collapse. That gives ED a concrete determinateness
  *mechanism* where the relational tradition (Whitehead, Rovelli, structural realism) has only an
  assumption.

- **The arrow is in the law, and has one source.** P11 supplies the irreversibility; **time
  homogeneity (P13) is the symmetric backdrop that forces the asymmetry to have a single origin**,
  rather than supplying one itself; V1's retardation carries it at the kernel level. The arrow is
  P11 + P13 + V1 jointly.

- **Becoming is sparse, and that is forced.** Commitment fires where there is indeterminacy to
  resolve, not at every tick — because dense commitment is the quantum Zeno limit, which would
  freeze all evolution and abolish ED's own quantum sector. The same sparse-becoming underwrites
  quantum coherence, gravitational time dilation, and preferred-frame safety: one structure, three
  faces.

- **Constants are global relational facts.** A constant's value is a property of the whole
  relational web, so a substrate cannot hand it to you in isolation — which makes ED's
  *value-inheritance* principled rather than apologetic. `G = c³ℓ_P²/ℏ` is the showcase: the form is
  derived, the value inherited through the single scale ℓ_ED ≡ ℓ_P.

- **Form yes, flesh as shadow.** ED's two signature commitments — orientation-blind selection and
  irreversible commitment — are *simultaneously* what make it a determinacy engine and what
  foreclose the determined continuum at the substrate layer. So ED supplies a domain's *form*, and
  the determined-continuum *flesh* (a field, a metric, a PDE) is its coarse-grained shadow.

## What ED claims — and what it doesn't

ED makes claims on two different axes.

**That it works — breadth without contradiction.** One small substrate generates the *form* of
physical law across quantum mechanics, gauge theory, gravity, black holes, computation,
entanglement, and fluids — internally consistent and honestly tiered, without breaking. The
evidence is not one decisive prediction; it is that a framework with this many independent chances
to fail hasn't. That doesn't make it true. It makes it worth a serious look.

**What it is — a category shift.** Beneath the physics, ED takes a position on what its results
*mean*: that constants are global relational facts, not micro-properties; that determinateness is a
substrate event (commitment), not a given; that the old choice between "things first" and "relations
first" is a false binary. ED did not derive the constants — it said what *kind of thing* they are,
and why a substrate cannot produce them in isolation.

A discipline runs through both axes: every result is a **conditional structural derivation given the
thirteen primitives and the two kernels**. Where the substrate fixes the *form* of a law but the
universe supplies the *number* (H₀, the Planck mass, mixing angles), the split is named:
**form-forced, value-inherited** — *form forced conditional on the postulates, never from a deeper
layer.* The primitives are posited; nothing downstream is forced from nothing.

## What ED is not

ED is **not** a Theory of Everything, **not** a derivation from nothing, and **not** a
forcing-from-zero-assumptions program. It is a postulational generative system whose case rests on
cross-domain reach and methodological honesty. The thirteen primitives are *posited*; the constants'
values are *inherited*, and the ontology explains why a substrate cannot produce them in isolation.
Key derivations remain open and are named as such: the full nonlinear Einstein field equations, the
numerical values of the constants, and a confirmed parameter-free novel prediction. ED's claim is
breadth without contradiction plus a defensible ontology — a program worth serious examination, not
a finished or confirmed theory.
