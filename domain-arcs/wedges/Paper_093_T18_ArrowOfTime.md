## Abstract

**Theorem T18 (Kernel-Level Arrow of Time):** The substrate kernel rule-types are forward-causal (retarded) by virtue of P11 (Commitment-irreversibility) at the substrate level. V1 finite-width retarded kernel (Paper_089) and V5 cross-chain correlation kernel (Paper_090) both impose temporal direction: substrate states at time $t$ depend on prior states (times $t' < t$) but not on future states. This kernel-level arrow is **structurally prior** to thermodynamic / radiative / cosmological arrows; those higher-level arrows inherit from the kernel-level arrow under DCGT coarse-graining. The result is FORM-FORCED by P11 + V1 + V5 retardation; specific phenomenological-arrow content is INHERITED.

## §3 The Theorem

### 3.1 Statement

**Theorem T18 (Kernel-Level Arrow of Time).** Let $K(x, t; x', t')$ be any substrate kernel rule-type (V1, V5, higher-order $V_n$). Under P11 commitment-irreversibility and P13 time homogeneity:

$$K(x, t; x', t') = 0 \quad \text{for } t' > t .$$

That is, the substrate kernel vanishes for future arguments. This defines the kernel-level arrow of time at substrate level.

### 3.2 The argument

By P11, substrate commitments are irreversible: once a substrate-level commitment is made at time $t_0$, it cannot be undone by any subsequent substrate state at $t > t_0$.

V1 (Paper_089) has retardation forced by P11: V1 propagates from $t' < t$ to $t$, not the reverse. V5 (Paper_090) has similar forward-causal structure: cross-chain correlations have finite memory in the **backward** direction only.

By P-T18-Kernel-Retardation, this property extends to all substrate kernel rule-types. Combined with P13 time homogeneity (translation invariance defining the arrow direction), the theorem follows.

### 3.3 Inheritance to higher-level arrows

By P-T18-Arrow-Inheritance, phenomenological arrows of time (thermodynamic second-law, radiative outgoing-wave, cosmological-expansion direction) inherit their directional content from the kernel-level arrow under DCGT coarse-graining.

This is **inheritance**, not derivation: each phenomenological arrow's specific content (entropy increase, radiation outgoing, expansion outward) is matched against standard physics; the substrate program supplies the kernel-level origin of the **direction**.

### 3.4 What is OPEN

Cosmological-arrow inheritance details (initial-condition fine-tuning, low-entropy-past problem) and quantum-measurement-arrow (whether measurement collapse inherits the kernel arrow or requires additional structure) are OPEN. The kernel-level theorem is established; phenomenological-arrow derivations are downstream.

## §4 Falsification Criteria

- **F1:** Substrate evidence that any substrate kernel rule-type is **advanced** (non-zero for $t' > t$) — refutes P-T18-Kernel-Retardation.
- **F2:** Empirical detection of a temporal-asymmetry phenomenon with arrow direction **opposite** to V1 / V5 retardation under DCGT — refutes P-T18-Arrow-Inheritance.
- **F3:** Demonstration that P11 commitment-irreversibility does NOT force V1 retardation — refutes the core mechanism (and would conflict with Paper_089).
