# The Two-Column Creed
### What Event Density affirms, and what it denies

For every deep puzzle in physics, Event Density does the same thing: it names one plain answer and refuses the received one. *Yes this, no that.* Written out, the pattern is not a grab-bag. It sorts under ED's two roots — **the arrow** (time is the act of commitment) and **the finite relational substrate** (a grained, related world with no true infinities). Everything below is one of those two, refracted through a mystery. Read straight down the left column and you have the whole worldview on one page.

---

## Root 1 — The Arrow: time is the act of commitment

- **Time.** *Fundamental* — the one-way act of commitment, the substrate's own direction. **Not** emergent, not a coordinate, not an illusion to be explained away. Everything else in this column is this, applied.
- **Measurement.** *The uncommitted becoming committed.* **Not** observer-magic, not a mystery, not a special role for minds. A measurement is a commitment; the "pointer basis" is simply the basis that commitment selects.
- **Entanglement.** *One shared, still-uncommitted state* — a hedge not yet cashed, and prior to space itself. **Not** spooky action at a distance, **not** one thing in many places. Nothing travels, because nothing has yet been decided.
- **The quantum-to-classical line.** *Crossed when a system's internal multiplicity — its complexity — reaches the threshold `M_crit`.* **Not** set by the environment alone. Mass is only a proxy for multiplicity, so the boundary is a *mass-versus-complexity tradeoff*: busy-and-light crosses where simple-and-heavy does. *(Falsifiable: ED places the crossing near 140–250 kDa in matter-wave interferometry — a pre-registered bet beyond today's ~25 kDa record. ED still shares ordinary environmental decoherence; the distinctive claim is that the irreversible commitment fires at an internal-complexity threshold.)*
- **Gravity.** *The same arrow, made geometric* — the khronon, emergent from the participation field (`g ~ 1/b`). **Not** a force, not a fifth interaction, not a field waiting to be quantized. Gravity is the shape of commitment's time.
- **The wavefunction.** *The between-commitments bookkeeping* — the smooth, reversible story that holds while nothing is being decided. **Not** the bottom layer. The thing everyone treats as fundamental is the emergent ledger.

## Root 2 — The finite relational substrate: a grained, related world, no true infinities

- **Dimensions.** *Three of space and one of time, forced* — and forced from several independent directions at once. **Not** tuned, not a landscape choice, not more, not fewer. The roads converge because 3D is the one place committed structure can persist.
- **Dark matter.** *Two answers, not one.* Galaxies need no particle — their extra pull is the substrate's own low-acceleration behavior (MOND); clusters and the early universe do carry a relic — a committed neutral remnant. So: **relic yes, halo-around-every-galaxy no.**
- **Singularities.** *An artifact of the smooth continuum, never reached by the grained substrate* — the infinity is where the map tears, not the territory. ED keeps Penrose's cold cyclic endpoints (CCC) and drops the singularity — yes, the result he won the prize for — **not** by calling his theorems wrong, but by denying the continuum they assume.
- **Information.** *Relational contrast* — a difference that makes a difference between two related things. **Not** a fundamental bit; the bit is its shadow, a convenient way to bill it. (Two is more fundamental than one.)

---

Read the left column alone and the affirmation is one sentence: **time is an act, space is grained and relational, and the mysteries dissolve** — because each one was an *uncommitted thing* mistaken for magic (entanglement, measurement, decoherence) or an *infinity* mistaken for a fact (singularities, halos, arbitrary dimensions, the perfect bit). The right column is just the received physics ED declines to inherit.

*Honest footing: most of these are standing structural results of the framework; the `M_crit` / 140–250 kDa boundary is a pre-registered, not-yet-tested prediction; and the singularity line is substrate regularization plus philosophical resonance with CCC, not a refutation of the singularity theorems (which are airtight in the continuum ED says is only the shadow).*
