# The Two-Column Creed
### What Event Density affirms, and what it denies

For every deep puzzle in physics, Event Density does the same thing: it names one plain answer and refuses the received one. *Yes this, no that.* Written out, the pattern is not a grab-bag. It sorts under ED's two roots — **the arrow** (time is the act of commitment) and **the finite relational substrate** (a grained, related world with no true infinities). Everything below is one of those two, refracted through a mystery. Read straight down the left column and you have the whole worldview on one page.

---

## Root 1 — The Arrow: time is the act of commitment

| ED affirms — *yes* | ED declines — *no* |
|---|---|
| **Time** — the one-way act of commitment, the substrate's own direction | emergent, a coordinate, an illusion to explain away |
| **Measurement** — the uncommitted becoming committed | observer-magic, a mystery, a role for minds |
| **Entanglement** — one shared, still-uncommitted state, prior to space | spooky action at a distance; one thing in many places |
| **The quantum→classical line** — crossed when internal complexity reaches `M_crit` | set by the environment alone |
| **Gravity** — the same arrow made geometric (the khronon, `g ~ 1/b`) | a force; a field waiting to be quantized |
| **The wavefunction** — the between-commitments bookkeeping | the bottom layer |

## Root 2 — The finite relational substrate: a grained, related world, no true infinities

| ED affirms — *yes* | ED declines — *no* |
|---|---|
| **Dimensions** — 3+1, forced from several directions at once | tuned; a landscape choice; more or fewer |
| **Dark matter** — MOND for galaxies **+** a committed relic for clusters/CMB | a particle halo around every galaxy |
| **Singularities** — a continuum artifact; keep the CCC cold endpoints | a real infinity in the world (the singularity) |
| **Information** — relational contrast | a fundamental bit (the bit is its shadow) |

---

Read the left column alone and the affirmation is one sentence: **time is an act, space is grained and relational, and the mysteries dissolve** — because each one was an *uncommitted thing* mistaken for magic (entanglement, measurement, decoherence) or an *infinity* mistaken for a fact (singularities, halos, arbitrary dimensions, the perfect bit). The right column is just the received physics ED declines to inherit.

*Honest footing: most of these are standing structural results of the framework; the `M_crit` / 140–250 kDa quantum-to-classical boundary is a pre-registered, not-yet-tested prediction (ED still shares ordinary environmental decoherence — the distinctive claim is that the irreversible commitment fires at an internal-complexity threshold); and the singularity line is substrate regularization plus philosophical resonance with CCC, not a refutation of the singularity theorems, which are airtight in the continuum ED says is only the shadow. The left column is a promissory note the framework earns, not a verdict asserted.*
