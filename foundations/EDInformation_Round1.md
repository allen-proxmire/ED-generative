## 1. The three-way information budget across a horizon

Across a `b→0` surface, information splits into three kinds with sharply different access:

- **Classical (V1 channel):** capacity **exactly 0** at the discrete `b=0` cut (A1). The interior is **classically inaccessible** — nothing crosses.
- **Quantum (V5 channel):** a **slow leak** at rate `~1/τ_V5` (the Hawking trickle, ED-10 R2). The interior dribbles out quantum-mechanically.
- **Determinability / common-cause (A1):** the interior and exterior may share a **common past** (correlation from a shared origin *before* the cut formed). This is information accessible by **inference, not transmission** — the exterior "already knows" something about the interior because they were once in contact.

This three-way split *is* the structure of the black-hole information picture: classically nothing escapes; Hawking radiation carries a quantum trickle; and the exterior's **entanglement-via-shared-origin** (A1 common-cause) is exactly the purification structure at the heart of the paradox. **A1's "observational, not transmission" is the resolution-ingredient: the interior's information was never *entirely* behind the horizon.**

## 3. Horizon information accounting — hidden, not lost

When a region becomes determinability-severed, is its information **lost** or **hidden**? In ED it is **hidden**, by two established facts: **P11 irreversibility** freezes the interior committed record (it persists, never erased), and **A1 common-cause** means the exterior still shares correlation with it via the shared past. So globally the information survives; it is merely **inaccessible to the exterior classically.**

From the exterior observer's view, that inaccessible information is **missing information = entropy**, and (ED-10 R3) it is **area-scaled**: because the interior is determinability-cut, the accessible/inaccessible boundary *is* the cut surface, so the missing information is **boundary-encoded** — `∝ A` (cut edges), not the interior volume. This is the **holographic structure of the missing-information entropy from A1 severance** — the *form* `S ∝ A`, with the coefficient value-inherited / EFE-dependent (no `S = A/4` claimed).

## 4. Information recovery

V5 leakage allows **partial** recovery (the quantum trickle out). But the deeper ED point is a **second, pre-existing recovery channel**: the **A1 common-cause correlation** — the exterior always shared some of the interior's information via the shared origin, so recovery does not require transmitting it all out. Recovery is therefore **two-channel**: (i) active V5 leakage, (ii) the standing shared-past correlation.

- **One-way (BH) horizon:** asymmetric — net outward leak; recovery via the outgoing quanta *plus* the common-cause correlation.
- **Two-way (cosmological) horizon:** symmetric; no hidden one-way interior, so no "recovery" problem — mutually severed but shared-origin-correlated.
- **Completeness:** whether leak + common-cause **fully** reconstruct the interior record (unitary recovery / the Page curve) is the **open question**. ED *reframes* it — the information was never wholly behind the horizon — but does not *resolve* it.

## 5. Global information architecture

The participation web (P02) partitions into **strata** (reciprocal-subgraph components; A1/A2); each horizon adds strata. Information is **intra-stratum shared** (common origin, correlated) and **inter-stratum severed** classically (capacity 0), though **common-cause-correlated** wherever strata shared a past. The key question — is information **globally conserved**? — has a precise, two-part answer:

- **Realized record: globally conserved.** P11 makes committed ρ monotone — the realized record only accumulates, never erases. The *facts that happened* are permanent across the whole web.
- **Counterfactual multiplicity: locally destroyed.** Commitment is **many-to-one** (M>1 → M=1; A1's interventional capacity 0) — the *unrealized alternatives* are gone at each commitment.

So ED **conserves the realized record globally and destroys counterfactual multiplicity locally.** This is exactly the unitarity tension of the information paradox, stated in substrate terms: ED is *record-conserving* but *not counterfactual-unitary*. Whether the V5 leak + A1 common-cause reconstruct the full realized record (record-unitarity) is the Page-curve question of §4.

## 7. Verdict

**ED has a coherent, EFE-free information architecture across horizons — and it reproduces the *structure* of the black-hole information picture while reframing the paradox.** Information at a horizon is **hidden, not lost** (P11 freezes the record; A1 common-cause persists); the exterior's **missing information is area-scaled** (boundary-encoded via A1 severance, holographic *form*, coefficient EFE-dependent); recovery runs through **two channels** (V5 leakage + the standing A1 common-cause correlation), asymmetric for one-way horizons and absent-as-a-problem for two-way; and global conservation holds for the **realized record** (P11 monotone) but **not** for **counterfactual multiplicity** (commitment many-to-one). ED therefore supplies all the **ingredients of a Page curve** — area-entropy that rises, leak-plus-common-cause that returns information — and reproduces its **qualitative shape**, while the **unitary completeness** (does it *exactly* purify) and all **numbers** remain open (the latter on EFE). The genuinely illuminating result: **A1's common-cause structure is the substrate form of "the interior's information was never entirely behind the horizon"** — the reframing the information paradox has been circling.
