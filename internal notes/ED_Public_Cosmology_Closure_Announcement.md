## What this is — and is not

### What the closure IS

- **A genuine substrate-graph derivation** of the form of $H_0$ from substrate primitives + standard physics inheritance, at the same tier as ED's existing M3 derivations (Newton's $G$, the deep-MOND acceleration scale $a_0$, the Hawking spectrum, etc.).
- **A reframing of the cosmological-constant problem** at substrate-graph level: the 120-OOM "hierarchy" becomes a substrate-parameter-value question rather than a quantum-field-theory mode-summation problem.
- **The largest cross-arc closure cascade in the corpus to date:** three Cosmology Arc papers + the six-projection ED-SC 4.x arc simultaneously upgrade to M3 with shared foundational dependency.

### What the closure is NOT

- **It does not derive $\Theta_{\mathrm{ED}}$ from sub-primitive content.** The substrate-parameter value is inherited at the primitive level, analogous to how Paper_027 inherits $\ell_P, \hbar, c$. Deriving $\Theta_{\mathrm{ED}}$ from sub-primitive structure remains a substrate-research-frontier question.
- **It does not eliminate the substrate-research-frontier tightening work.** Per-projection scaling exponent derivation, multi-route convergence relation derivation, and substrate-parameter-value derivation all remain as substantive future work.
- **It does not predict a new $H_0$ value that disagrees with observation.** The closure delivers the form-derivation; the numerical value is consistent with observed $H_0 \approx 70$ km/s/Mpc at the substrate-parameter-INHERITED value $\Theta_{\mathrm{ED}}^{\mathrm{Planck-units}} \approx 10^{-122}$.
- **It does not change ED's broader honest framing.** ED remains in the substrate-ontology research-program lineage ('t Hooft cellular-automaton interpretation; Wolfram Ruliad; causal-set program), not in the closed-proof operational-reconstruction lineage. The methodology discipline (form-FORCED / value-INHERITED per Paper_095) is unchanged.
