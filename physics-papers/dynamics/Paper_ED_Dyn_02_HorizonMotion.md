## §5 Falsification Criteria

- **F1 (sharpest):** Observation of cosmological equation-of-state values outside the substrate-side admitted set $\{-1, 0, 1/3\}$ (and their additive composites) at a level that cannot be absorbed into mixed-substrate composition or standard observational uncertainties. - **F2:** Observation of horizon kinematics deviating from standard Friedmann-based predictions at a level beyond standard observational uncertainty in any cosmological phase. Refutes §3.4 phase-by-phase table.

- **F3:** Demonstration that V1 + V5 kernel structure cannot supply both relativistic-analog and non-relativistic-analog substrate content — refuting Q1 + Q2 from below rather than above (making the chain-class distinction impossible substrate-graph). Refutes §3.2 + §3.3 candidate identifications and forces a different substrate-graph mechanism.

- **F4:** Refutation of DCGT hydrodynamic-window applicability in RDE/MDE at standard cosmological scales (e.g., discovery of substrate-scale physics that interferes with coarse-graining). Refutes §3.4.
