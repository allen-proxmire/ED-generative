## Abstract

Event Density postulates thirteen substrate primitives (Paper_087). This paper asks the minimality question the position paper itself flags (§8.4): does the set contain a derivable subset? We run a structured reduction audit — for each primitive, is it a **facet** of another primitive, **presupposed** by it (logically prior), or **independent**? — and reach a compact result. **(1)** The primitives split cleanly into three roles: the *things* commitment acts on (participation, distinguishable channels, a complex amplitude), the *arena* it runs on (spatial homogeneity, three dimensions, scale, kinds), and *commitment itself* with its facets (forward-causal chains, polarity-transport, the stability landscape, and — shown here — time-homogeneity). Commitment (P11) is the sole **process**: every dynamical and directional structure in ED is commitment acting. **(2)** Two reductions tighten the count. The bandwidth and phase primitives **fuse** into one complex amplitude, because this program established they are complementary conjugates and the amplitude field is ℂ. And **time-homogeneity reduces to commitment**: since commitment is the sole process and is stated as one fixed rule, iterating that rule yields time-homogeneous laws, so "no privileged moment" is just "the one rule is time-fixed." **(3)** Time-homogeneity does *not*, however, merge with spatial homogeneity into one spacetime symmetry: ED's time carries the arrow and its space does not, so a unified translation symmetry is emergent, not primitive. The net honest floor is **3 substrate + 4 arena + 1 commitment**, and its shortest faithful description is a pair: **commitment and participation**. The capstone: ED has a spatial stage but **no separate temporal stage** — space is a genuine arena, while time is entirely the process. Three-dimensionality is the one arena member with a live (conditional) route back to commitment, via a linking argument whose premise remains open. Throughout, the reduction is of primitive *structure*, not empirical values, and it introduces no new primitive.

## 1. Introduction

Event Density is a substrate ontology built on thirteen postulated primitives, enumerated authoritatively in the position paper (Paper_087). The position paper is careful to state that the set is postulated, not derived, and it flags its own minimality as open — §8.4 offers a falsifier sentence: *"Demonstration that the canonical 13 contains a derivable subset (e.g., P13 derivable from P11 + P03, or P06 derivable from P03 + P07) would reduce the primitive count without changing downstream content."*

This paper takes up that invitation and reports the result of a systematic reduction audit. The question is not whether the primitives can be derived from *outside* ED (they are the foundational layer, by construction) but whether, *inside* the set, some primitives are facets of others. The answer is yes, and the shape of the answer is more informative than the count: the primitives organize into three roles, one of which — commitment — turns out to be the only source of process in the theory, and to absorb the entire temporal side of the arena while leaving the spatial side genuinely irreducible.

The result gives ED a compact, honest description. It is not "ED is one primitive" (that is false, and the paper says where it fails). It is a *pair*: commitment, and the participation it acts on, over a spatial stage. Section 2 states the method. Sections 3–6 run the audit and the two reductions. Section 7 states the floor and the capstone. Sections 8–10 give the arrow-as-keystone synthesis, limitations, and falsifiers.

## 2. Method: The Reduction Audit

The primitives (Paper_087 §5, abbreviated): P01 event-density-layer existence; P02 participation as primitive relation; P03 channel + locus indexing with spatial homogeneity; P04 bandwidth (non-negative additive scalar); P05 polarity-transport along edges; P06 spatial dimension (D = 3+1); P07 distinguishable channels; P08 substrate scale ℓ_ED; P09 polarity (U(1) phase); P10 rule-type multiplicity; P11 commitment irreversibility; P12 stability landscape; P13 time homogeneity.

For each primitive we ask, against the canonical statement of commitment (P11: *"at commitment events, a chain's multi-channel participation collapses to single-channel, with the un-selected channels' phase content randomized; irreversibly"*):

- **FACET** — definitionally part of, or entailed by, another primitive ⟹ it reduces.
- **PRESUPPOSED** — the other primitive's definition already requires it ⟹ it is logically *prior* and cannot reduce to that primitive (the primitive needs it to be stated at all).
- **INDEPENDENT** — neither entailed by nor presupposed by the others.

The test is deliberately conservative: "does many jobs" is not "is the only primitive," and a primitive that P11's own definition references cannot be a facet of P11 — it is prior to it. The audit is a structural analysis of the primitive statements, not an empirical experiment; where a step rests on a measured or separately-derived result, that is flagged in the load-bearing table (§2.5).

## 3. The Three-Role Split, and Commitment as the Sole Process

The audit produces a clean three-way partition.

**Role 1 — the things commitment acts on (PRESUPPOSED by P11, hence prior):** P02 participation (P11 collapses *participation*), P04 bandwidth (the collapse is bandwidth-weighted — the Born-rule content), P07 channels (P11 collapses *to a channel*, among *distinguishable* ones), P09 phase (P11 *randomizes un-selected phases*). These four are exactly the objects P11's own definition references; commitment cannot reduce them, it presupposes them. They are also, together, the participation amplitude `P_K = √b_K e^{iπ_K}` on channel K — commitment is what commits it.

**Role 2 — the arena (INDEPENDENT of the arrow):** P03 spatial homogeneity, P06 three dimensions, P08 scale, P10 rule-type multiplicity, and (before §5) P13 time-homogeneity. These are the stage the process runs on — spatial, scale, and kind facts the arrow neither creates nor presupposes.

**Role 3 — commitment and its facets (the process):** P11 commitment itself; P01 chains, whose *forward-causal ordering* is the arrow applied to participation-events; P05 polarity-transport, whose *forward-directedness* is the arrow carrying the phase; P12 the stability landscape, whose *constituents* are the Role-1 nouns and whose *dynamics* (descend Σ) is realized by commitment moving the chain.

The refined statement, which the rest of the paper sharpens:

> **Commitment (P11) is the only PROCESS primitive.** Everything static — the nouns it acts on and the arena it runs on — is not commitment and does not reduce to it. But everything dynamical or directional — forward-causal chains, polarity-transport, the descent of the stability landscape, and (as physics) measurement and the pointer basis, matter/antimatter, gravity's causal direction, information preservation, the hypercharge reference — *is* commitment acting. Commitment is the single source of process; the other primitives are its objects and its stage.

This is why commitment appears everywhere in ED: not because it is the only primitive, but because it is the only *process*, so any dynamical structure, followed to its root, is commitment.

## 4. First Reduction: Bandwidth and Phase Fuse into One Complex Amplitude

The four Role-1 nouns look like four primitives, but two of them fuse. Bandwidth `b_K = |P_K|²` and phase `π_K = arg P_K` are the modulus and phase of one complex number `P_K ∈ ℂ`. In isolation that is only polar coordinates, not a reduction (a complex number still has two real degrees of freedom). What makes it a genuine fusion is two results established elsewhere in this program:

1. **Complementarity.** Bandwidth and phase are complementary: a which-channel (bandwidth) commitment destroys phase coherence, and a definite relative phase forces equal bandwidths. They cannot be jointly sharp. Complementary observables are conjugate aspects of *one* object (as position and momentum are of one state), not two independently-specifiable primitives.
2. **The amplitude field is ℂ.** ED's amplitude field is complex — the U(1) phase (P09) rules out a real field, and composite structure rules out quaternionic. So the fundamental object is a complex amplitude, whose modulus and phase are its two conjugate faces.

So P04 and P09 are not two primitives; they are the modulus and phase of one complex participation amplitude — bound by complementarity, which is precisely what distinguishes "one conjugate object" from "two independent knobs." This is a real 2 → 1.

The other two Role-1 nouns stay distinct. P07 (channels) is the *basis* the amplitude lives in — the distinguishable options, the pointer basis — separable from the amplitude values (channels are distinct even at equal bandwidth and phase). P02 (participation) is the primitive *relation* — a chain couples to a channel, here, now — the addressing the amplitude is content of, not the amplitude value. So the substrate nouns compress from four to three: **participation (a relation), channels (a basis), and the complex amplitude (their content).**

## 5. Second Reduction: Time-Homogeneity Is a Facet of Commitment

The arena has two "no privileged spot" members — spatial homogeneity (P03: no privileged locus) and time-homogeneity (P13: no privileged moment). The tempting move is to merge them into one spacetime-translation symmetry. **ED must refuse this, and refusing it is a distinctive result.** In ED, time carries the arrow (commitment) and space does not; P13 (no privileged moment) is the complementary partner of P11's privileged *direction*, and space has no such directional partner. So "homogeneity of space" and "homogeneity of time" are homogeneities of two structurally different axes, not one symmetry in two guises. A unified spacetime-translation symmetry — the continuum's Poincaré invariance — is therefore *emergent / coarse-grained*, not primitive: ED's substrate carries **less** symmetry than its smooth limit, by design, because of the arrow.

But P13 still is not an independent *arena* primitive — it reduces to commitment, and by a cleaner route than the position paper's "P11 + P03" guess:

1. **Commitment is the sole process** (§3): nothing else generates time-evolution.
2. **Canonical commitment is one fixed rule**, not a time-indexed family — there is no `t` in "at commitment events, participation collapses irreversibly"; it is a single operation.
3. Iterating one fixed rule produces laws identical at every time — a privileged moment would require the rule to differ there, and there is only one rule.

So **time-homogeneity is "the one process is time-fixed," which is already contained in commitment being a single rule.** P13 is a facet of P11, and it needs only P11 — the spatial-homogeneity input P03 is not required (sharpening the paper's `P13 = P11 + P03` to `P13 ⊂ P11`).

One boundary, scoped honestly: "no privileged moment" is about the *laws*, not the *state*. The arrow may give time a special *initial* moment (a first commitment), but a special initial state is a boundary condition, not a law-inhomogeneity — the laws remain the same everywhere. So the arrow's possible beginning is a separate initial-condition matter and does not threaten `P13 ⊂ P11`, which is about law-invariance, and law-invariance is exactly the fixedness of the one rule.

## 6. The One That Does Not Reduce (Yet): Three Dimensions

Three-dimensionality (P06) is the hardest arena member, and the honest verdict is that it is **selected, not derived** — it stays a primitive. Three independent arguments land on 3: the internal amplitude dimension (a gauge family's channels are mutually-orthogonal complex vectors, and the max in ℂ^d is d, so the observed families {1,2,3} fix d = 3); the holographic reach law (only d = 3 gives the observed `g ~ 1/b`); and a linking argument. Three independent landings on the same number is strong internal coherence, but each is anchored on an input (the observed gauge content, observed gravity, or a structural premise), so this is selection, not derivation.

The linking argument is the one genuine *derivation candidate*, and it routes 3D back to commitment. A link (two loops threaded) is the only structure that holds an order against continuous rearrangement, and stable linking of one-dimensional loops exists in exactly three dimensions (2D cannot form a link; 4D unravels any link — rigorous topology). Read against the primitives, its premises are commitment-side: "committed order must be stable against continuous rearrangement" *is* commitment's irreversibility (a committed order is permanent, hence topologically protected), and "1D loops" *are* the forward-causal chains. So the candidate is: {1D chains + committed order topologically stable + stable 1D-linking unique to 3D} ⟹ d = 3 — which, *if it holds*, forces three-dimensionality from commitment needing to hold stable topological records of 1D chains.

The candidate rests on one open premise: that ED holds its committed order by linking *specifically*. Its status is half-settled. The cheap alternative — order held by node labels or timestamps — is **ruled out** on the certified simulator: relabel the substrate with physics fixed and the committed order barely moves (Kendall τ ≈ 0.96 versus 0.5 for a label-scramble; the committing-node set is invariant), so the order is geometry-held. And the structure that would hold it is reachable (the multi-chain participation graph can be intrinsically linked). What remains open is isolating that the geometric holder is *linking* specifically, which requires a three-dimensional substrate to even ask, and so is not testable on the certified two-dimensional simulator — an honest hard limit, shared with the open curvature-emergence derivation. So P06 stays a primitive, with a grounded, commitment-connected, conditional route back.

## 7. The Floor and the Capstone

Collecting the audit and the two reductions: the substrate nouns compress 4 → 3 (§4); time-homogeneity leaves the arena for commitment's facets (§5); the arena's remaining members are spatial. The honest minimal count is:

$$\boxed{\;3\ \text{substrate (participation, channels, complex amplitude)} \;+\; 4\ \text{arena (spatial homogeneity, 3D, scale, kinds)} \;+\; 1\ \text{commitment (with chains, transport, stability-landscape, time-homogeneity as its facets)}\;}$$

and its shortest faithful description is a **pair**:

> **The shortest way to describe Event Density is commitment and participation** — the sole process, and the sole thing it acts on. The amplitude on channels is what participation carries; the arena is the presupposed spatial stage. This is not a slogan placed over the physics; it is where the reduction landed.

Two features are worth stating plainly. First, it is a *pair*, not a single primitive — faithful to ED's relational stance that a relation is prior to an isolated thing (participation is already a relation: a chain, to a channel, here, now; commitment is the act on that relation). Second, and the capstone:

> **ED has a spatial stage but no separate temporal stage.** The remaining arena is purely spatial — three dimensions, spatial homogeneity, scale, kinds — with no arrow. Time contributes nothing to the arena: its two facets are commitment's direction and commitment's fixed-rule homogeneity. There is no "time" sitting in the arena beside space, because what we call time is commitment happening.

This is why "commitment and participation" is the honest shortest form and not an oversimplification: one does not need a third word for time, because time is the commitment. The arena is spatial; the process is temporal; and the process is the arrow.

## 8. Discussion: Commitment as the Keystone of ED's Physics

The reduction explains a pattern visible across ED's derivations: commitment is not merely one primitive among many but the single load-bearing one, because it is the only process. Followed to their roots, ED's physical structures are commitment acting:

- **Time and irreversibility** are commitment directly.
- **Measurement and the pointer basis** — commitment resolves only in the channel basis, so ED is a preferred-basis theory because the arrow selects the basis (einselection is primitive here).
- **The measurement-repeatability (covering-law) content** — irreversibility locks a committed channel, which is the repeatability a quantum-logic reconstruction needs.
- **Matter/antimatter (C)** — first-arrival commitment selects the charge conjugation, the one chirality-adjacent thing ED fixes natively.
- **Gravity's causal direction** — the one-way black-hole horizon versus the two-way cosmological one is set by the arrow.
- **Information preservation** — the same irreversibility that forbids reversal forbids erasure: information is preserved as the permanent record of every commitment.
- **The single hypercharge U(1)** — polarity is phased against the one external flow, the commitment flow, so there is one gauged U(1) because there is one arrow to phase against.

Each of these has its own support elsewhere in the program; assembled, they are the physical face of §3's structural claim that commitment is the sole process. This synthesis is an **account**, not a theorem (preamble 4): it explains *why* commitment is everywhere without claiming the other primitives are derivable from it — they are its objects and its stage, and the paper has argued (§3–§6) exactly where commitment stops.

- **The keystone synthesis is an account.** §8 assembles per-job results into a unifying picture; it is not a single derivation and is labelled interpretation.
- **Values are untouched.** Nothing here reduces the empirical value layer; the minimality is of primitive structure only.

## 10. Falsification Criteria

### 10.1 A Role-1 noun is not presupposed by commitment

**Falsifier sentence:** *A demonstration that participation, channels, or the amplitude can be stated without commitment presupposing them — or that one of them is in fact a facet of commitment — would overturn the three-role split (§3).*

### 10.2 Bandwidth and phase are independent primitives

**Falsifier sentence:** *A demonstration that bandwidth and phase are jointly sharp (no complementarity) or that the amplitude field is not ℂ would break the 4 → 3 fusion (§4) and restore them as two primitives.*

### 10.3 Time-homogeneity is independent of commitment

**Falsifier sentence:** *A demonstration that ED's laws could be time-inhomogeneous while commitment remains a single fixed rule — or that commitment must be a time-indexed family — would falsify `P13 ⊂ P11` (§5).*

### 10.4 Space and time share one primitive homogeneity

**Falsifier sentence:** *A demonstration that ED's substrate carries a single unified spacetime-translation symmetry at the primitive level (not merely emergently) would falsify the space/time asymmetry claim (§5) and collapse the arrow's distinction between the two axes.*

### 10.5 Three-dimensionality is genuinely derived (or genuinely free)

**Falsifier sentence:** *Either a completed derivation of P06 (closing the linking premise) or a demonstration that no consistency argument selects 3 would move P06 off its current "selected, not derived" status (§6).*
