## Abstract

Quantum Darwinism (QD) — the redundant imprinting of a system's pointer-basis information into many environment fragments, the mechanism of classical objectivity — is constructed inside ED and shown to be the **fourth face of the single V5 budget** of `Paper_V5UnifiedBudget`, with one decisive refinement: the budget bifurcates on P11 commitment. `Paper_090` already draws the line (committed structure vs uncommitted amplitude in horizon straddling); with P11 no-erasure and the A1 result (correlations persist at zero channel capacity), it follows structurally that **a committed record costs no V5 budget**. Consequences: (i) accumulated classical redundancy is *unbounded* — everyday objectivity, at Riedel–Zurek magnitudes, is not merely tolerated but explained (records are free precisely because they are committed); (ii) what the budget caps is **live** redundancy: the number of environment fragments simultaneously holding *uncommitted* correlation with the system, `N_live ≤ W_max/w̄`, and hence a record-formation bandwidth `dR/dt ≲ N_live/τ_commit` through any single locus. Under a declared postulate (P-QD-LiveWeight), consistent with (though not forced by) `Paper_058`'s error-correction accounting and drawing on the same budget, the cap becomes a **ceiling on coherent branching-state (GHZ-type) width**. This yields the paper's weapon: the Class-C error-suppression plateau and the GHZ-width ceiling are *one budget spent the same way*, so ED predicts they appear at **commensurate live-correlation content** (ratio O(1)); observing either pins the other. Floors are evaluated under the pre-registered content rule of `Paper_ProxyConversionDoctrine` (per-locus live content, certification-discounted): the 120-qubit F=0.56 GHZ yields a guaranteed floor of order 10⁰–10¹ bits per locus — low enough that the certified frontier probes the broadcast-side wall directly. Topological codes load loci O(1) and floor weakly; if that reading of 058's α holds, the code-distance leg transfers entirely to broadcast-type redundancy (open item, doctrine §3.4). Standard physics has neither ceiling.

## 2. The two kinds of cross-chain correlation

`Paper_090` draws the split once, in the horizon-specific §6.4 (ER=EPR): cross-chain correlations straddling a horizon have "**committed structure (P11) interior-confined and uncommitted-entanglement amplitude straddling**." That clause is horizon-local; this paper **generalizes** it into an accounting principle (the generalization, not the clause, is what the theorem below rests on). V5's correlation content bifurcates on commitment:

- **Live (uncommitted):** coherent amplitude relations between chains — entanglement, branch superposition structure. Finite-memory (`τ_V5` decay unless sustained), and the thing every face of the unified budget caps: monogamy's pair-tangles (065), QEC's redundant encoding coherence (058), horizon-straddling entanglement (050). All live.
- **Committed:** P11-recorded correlation. Irreversible (no erasure), persistent without any sustaining channel. The **A1 result** (run on the certified V5-free kinetic lattice-gas substrate) demonstrates the committed side directly: controlled channel capacity between separated regions is *exactly zero* while common-cause correlations persist — persistence of committed correlation requires no transmission channel at all. (A1 supports the principle; being V5-free, it does not by itself measure V5 weight.)

**Theorem (accounting, D-structural).** *A P11-committed record consumes no V5 budget.* The budget `W_max` bounds `∫K_V5` (065 §3.1 — stated there without a live/committed qualifier; this theorem restricts it to live content); a committed record is not kernel content but recorded structure (generalizing 090 §6.4's committed/uncommitted split); it persists without any channel (A1) and cannot be erased to "refund" anything (P11). The generalization is also the *unique* corpus-consistent accounting: if committed records drew budget, Riedel–Zurek-scale everyday redundancy (§3) would exhaust any finite `W_max` in daylight. Hence the budget constrains correlation *formation and coherent maintenance*, never the stock of committed records.

## 3. First consequence: objectivity is unbounded, and that is a derivation, not an escape

Riedel–Zurek: a 1 μm dust grain in sunlight has its position imprinted ~10⁸ times per μs, growing linearly without bound. Under a naive "V5 caps all cross-chain correlation" reading, ED would be falsified by daylight. The accounting theorem turns this around: **accumulated redundancy is unbounded because records are committed**, and committed structure is exactly the category the budget does not see. ED thus *derives* the QD phenomenology of everyday objectivity (huge, ever-growing redundancy) from the same commitment primitive that gives it the arrow of time. Classicality is cheap in ED for a principled reason: the classical *is* the committed.

## 4. Second consequence: the live cap

### 4.1 Per-locus cap on simultaneous uncommitted fragments

By P-V5-Budget + P04 additivity (065 §3.1–3.2), the system chain's total live pairwise weight partitions across partners: `W_S = Σ_i W_{S,E_i} ≤ W_max`. If a fragment "in the act of being imprinted" (correlated with `S`, not yet committed) holds mean pairwise weight `w̄`, then the number of simultaneously live, *directly*-correlated fragments obeys

$$
N_{\rm live} \;\le\; W_{\max}/\bar w .
$$

### 4.2 Record-formation bandwidth

Each record occupies live budget for the commitment window `τ_commit` (formation to P11 event). A single system locus therefore has a bounded record-formation *rate*:

$$
\frac{dR}{dt} \;\lesssim\; \frac{N_{\rm live}}{\tau_{\rm commit}} \;=\; \frac{W_{\max}}{\bar w\,\tau_{\rm commit}} .
$$

Total `R_δ(t)` is unbounded (§3); the *throughput* through one locus is not. Secondary proliferation (fragment-to-fragment copying, records of records) loads the fragments' budgets, not `S`'s, so global redundancy growth also evades any single-locus limit — again consistent with Riedel–Zurek, where each photon scatters once and commits fast (`τ_D` tiny, so live occupancy at the grain stays small at any instant).

### 4.3 Topology caveat (stated, load-bearing)

The per-locus cap constrains **hub topology**: many fragments simultaneously live-correlated *directly* with `S` — the Darwinism-natural configuration (photons all scatter off the grain), and the direct-coupling configuration of QD experiments. It does **not**, by itself, cap a state prepared through a *path* (ladder) circuit, where each chain carries only ~2 pairwise links. Converting the per-locus cap into a ceiling on the *state* requires §5.

## 5. P-QD-LiveWeight, and its consistency with 058

**P-QD-LiveWeight (declared):** in a sustained uncommitted state, *all* live cross-chain correlation — including branch-basis (pointer-classical) correlation, not only pairwise entanglement measures — consumes pairwise V5 weight, bounded below by some `w_min > 0` per bit of pairwise correlation content.

Why this is not free-floating — stated at its honest strength: `Paper_058` accounts per redundant *channel* ("substrate-level cross-chain coverage," `B_cross ≈ α·R_C`) and is silent on the quantum-information character of the content; at saturation, extra channels "cannot be coherently correlated." Stabilizer-encoded redundancy has near-zero pairwise tangles, so **on any reading of 058 where the plateau mechanism operates for hub-like loading, some non-tangle live correlation consumes `B_cross`** — which is P-QD-LiveWeight's content. And `Paper_V5UnifiedBudget` §5 showed `B_cross,max = W_max` (ρ_loc = 1, one budget). So the postulate is *consistent with* 058's accounting and shares its budget; it is **not forced by it** — 058 never characterizes its content as branch-basis correlation, and the encoding fork (`Paper_ProxyConversionDoctrine` §3.2–3.4) means the QEC family that clearly exemplifies large per-locus branch loading is broadcast/repetition-type, i.e. GHZ-type itself. The postulate stands as declared (Preamble 3), motivated by shared-budget consistency, and falls with F-QD-1.

**Consequence.** An `(N+1)`-chain GHZ-type branching state (each fragment perfectly pointer-correlated with `S`, all uncommitted) carries ~`N` bits of live pairwise correlation *at every chain* (each pair has unit pointer mutual information). Under P-QD-LiveWeight every chain's budget pays ~`N·w_min`, so the state has a global **width ceiling**:

$$
N_{\rm GHZ}^{\max} \;\sim\; W_{\max}/w_{\min} ,
$$

independent of preparation topology (the *sustained state's* correlation pattern is symmetric, whatever circuit built it).

## 6. The weapon: two ceilings, one budget, commensurate scales

The Class-C plateau (058; report §14 weapon #4) and the GHZ-width ceiling are the *same* `W_max` spent on the *same category* of live redundant correlation. Therefore ED predicts, at the form level with an O(1) projection ratio `r_QD` (extending `Paper_V5UnifiedBudget`'s R2 to a fourth entry):

> **The QEC error-suppression plateau and the coherent branching-width ceiling appear at commensurate live-correlation content.** Neither can sit at astronomically different scale from the other. Observing either pins the other through the unified budget (`1 : 1 : 0.88 : r_QD`).

**Standard physics has neither ceiling.** Error suppression continues indefinitely with code distance (given below-threshold hardware); GHZ width is limited only by engineering noise, with no fundamental wall. A confirmed plateau in one arena *plus* a confirmed ceiling in the other at commensurate content is a two-arena signature no noise model naturally produces.

## 8. Falsification Criteria

- **F-QD-1 (the sharp one):** a confirmed Class-C error-suppression plateau at live content `X` together with certified GHZ-type states at width ≫ `X`-equivalent content (or vice versa) — kills the single-accounting claim (P-QD-LiveWeight + ρ_loc = 1 jointly).

- **F-QD-3:** observed saturation of *accumulated* classical redundancy in a growing environment — contradicts §3 (ED predicts no such saturation; this falsifier runs *against* the naive reading of this arc, deliberately).

- **F-form:** GHZ width and code distance both growing indefinitely with no anomalous wall as engineering improves — the shared prediction of standard physics; sustained enough, it inverts §6 from weapon to failed bet (this is the honest exposure, same exposure as weapon #4 itself).

## 9. Position Statement

Quantum Darwinism assembles inside ED from standing parts, and it splits the V5 budget along the commitment line: **committed records are free, live redundancy is budgeted.** The free half derives everyday classical objectivity at Riedel–Zurek magnitudes from P11 itself. The budgeted half yields a per-locus live-fragment cap and record-formation bandwidth (derived, form level), and — under P-QD-LiveWeight, whose accounting the Class-C plateau already commits ED to — a ceiling on coherent branching-state width **commensurate with the error-correction plateau**: two ceilings, one budget, ratio O(1), neither existing in standard physics. Certified broadcast-type states (currently the 120-qubit GHZ, guaranteed floor order 10⁰–10¹ bits/locus) put a rising public floor under the budget when converted under the pre-registered content rule; topological codes floor weakly, and if their per-locus load is O(1) in depth the code-distance leg transfers entirely to broadcast-type redundancy (open, doctrine §3.4). The absolute scale stays inherited; the commensurability is the falsifiable content.

**What this paper claims:** the dictionary; the accounting theorem; unbounded objectivity; the live cap and bandwidth (form); the conditional width ceiling and its commensurability with the Class-C plateau. **What it does not:** any absolute ceiling value, any cap on accumulated classical redundancy, any unconditional GHZ bound.
