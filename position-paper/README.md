# Position Paper — Folder Guide *(the front door & program-level statements)*

**What this folder is.** The corpus's **public-facing entry layer**: the whole-program overviews, the whitepaper that is the literal "front door," the two philosophical position pieces (what kind of thing ED *is*), the canonical primitives position paper, and the honesty/reach synthesis. If someone asks "what is Event Density and where do I start?", the answer lives here — not in the 150 technical papers, which this folder maps *into*.

**State** *(these are statements-of-the-program and orientation docs, not new results)*:
- **The front door is [ED_WHITEPAPER.md](ED_WHITEPAPER.md)** — it self-identifies as such ("the 100+ paper corpus is the building behind it"), is the broadest in scope, and carries its own corpus-navigation section.
- **The canonical primitive enumeration lives in [paper_ED_Framework_13_Primitive_Generative_System.md](paper_ED_Framework_13_Primitive_Generative_System.md)** — this *is* the position paper that `physics-papers/foundations/Paper_087_13Primitives.md` corresponds to (same axioms, P01–P13). Every other doc that lists the primitives (Whitepaper §2, Program Review §3.2) is recapping this one. It is the only `.md` here **without** a PDF build.
- **There are two distinct "position papers"** — don't conflate them: the *axiomatic* one (Framework / Paper_087) states the primitives; the *philosophical* one ([Contrast-First Ontology](paper_ED_Contrast_First_Ontology.md)) argues what ED is at the level of ontology ("nothing here is a result in physics; it is a statement of ontology").
- **These are actively-revised documents** — the Whitepaper and Program Review carry internal dated corrections (e.g. the 2026-07-05 Route-A "closure" tightening). Quote their status language; don't paraphrase it as settled.

## The two walls (what *Form and Flesh* actually argues)

The load-bearing point is that ED's "no's" are **heterogeneous and must not transfer**:

- **Wall 1 — the coarse-graining wall (mostly OPEN):** the withheld flesh is a *determined continuum object* one layer up (a field, a metric, a PDE). Members: **charge (B4), gravity (GR-I/II), the continuum limit.** Nothing proves these unreachable; within the wall there's a trichotomy of *how* the flesh is withheld (reachable by admissible extension / reachable only by leaving ED / one DCGT layer up and open).
- **Wall 2 — the finite-memory wall (the only PROVEN "no"):** the withheld flesh is uncomputable / infinite-memory — **primality** (the Möbius sign), anchored externally to Sarnak's parity barrier.
- **A1 (channel capacity) sits slightly apart** — its withheld flesh is *provably absent by locality* (capacity exactly zero), so it's cleanly neither wall.

You cannot infer "primality is unreachable, therefore Maxwell is" nor "GR's metric was derived, therefore primality is reachable." Heterogeneous walls — see [[project_two_walls_heterogeneous_no]].
