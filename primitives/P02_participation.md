# P02 — Participation as primitive relation

**Canonical primitive of the ED Generative System.**
**Position paper reference:** `position-paper/paper_ED_Framework_13_Primitive_Generative_System.md` §1.1.

## Canonical statement

Chains (persistent micro-event sequences) participate in channels as a primitive substrate-level relation. Participation is the substrate's mechanism for chain-channel interaction.

Without P02, no participation measure can be defined; the complex polar carrier $P_K = \sqrt{b_K}\,e^{i\pi_K}$ (Paper #1) has no domain, and the QM-kinematics arc cannot start.
