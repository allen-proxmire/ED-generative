**Canonical primitive of the ED Generative System.**
**Position paper reference:** `position-paper/paper_ED_Framework_13_Primitive_Generative_System.md` §1.2.

## Canonical statement

The substrate's spatial axis is $\mathbb{R}^3$, plus one time dimension. The substrate is intrinsically 3+1-dimensional.

## Honest framing

P06 is **posited**, not derived. The corpus contains *multiple independent consistency arguments* for $D = 3+1$ (PDE-uniqueness axioms in NS-1 / Paper_075, spinor-bundle structure in the Dirac arc, acoustic-metric signature in the gravity arc), but these are downstream consistency checks rather than derivations of $D$ from a deeper substrate principle. ED does not derive the dimensionality of space.

## Audit verdict

LOAD-BEARING. The 3+1 commitment is invoked wherever dimensional analysis enters — NS-1 dimensional forcing (Paper 075), the holographic participation-count bound (Paper 025), Newton's $G$ structural form (Paper 027), the Dirac $g = 2$ result, and the acoustic-metric construction (Paper 035).

P06 has no concept-file counterpart. The dimensionality of space is the most "axiomatic" of the canonical primitives — it has no underlying ontological-concept decomposition; it is simply posited as the dimension count.
