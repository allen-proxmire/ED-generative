## Canonical statement

The substrate's dynamical primitive is invariant under time translation; the substrate has no preferred absolute time origin. Events on the substrate are **primitively discrete** (the manifold is not infinitely refinable).

P13 supplies:
- $H_0$ as a cosmological-rate parameter (Paper 028, Paper 029 — $a_0 = cH_0/(2\pi)$).
- The temporal-translation invariance entering Stone's theorem on the participation manifold.
- The substrate-level commitment that time is *not* a continuous parameter at the substrate scale (events are discrete; continuous time emerges via DCGT coarse-graining).

## Joint structure with P11

P13 (time-homogeneity) and P11 (commitment-irreversibility) together supply the arrow of time. P13 alone is reversible (translation-invariance is a symmetry, not a direction); P11 alone needs a temporal framework to be defined against. Both are required; V1's retardation property (Paper 093 / T18) inherits from their joint structure.
