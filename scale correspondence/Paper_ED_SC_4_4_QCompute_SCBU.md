- **F3: Cross-platform universality fails.** If empirically-mapped matter-wave $\mathcal{M}_{\mathrm{crit}}$ (from Paper_056 wall) disagrees with empirically-mapped qubit-array $\mathcal{M}_{\mathrm{crit}}$ (from Paper_057 or 058 wall) at the substrate level — i.e., the two platforms probe **different** boundaries — P-Cross-Platform-Universality (Paper_060) fails, and the SCBU-projection identification's universality content fails.

- **F4: SCBU postulate refuted.** Universal failure mode for the ED-SC 4.x arc: refutation of P-Substrate-Cosmology-Unified eliminates the anchor for all projection-extensions.

- **F5: IR-regime scaling inconsistent with kernel cascade.** If Paper_091's kernel cascade structure precludes a smooth transition from IR-hydrodynamic regime to IR-Q-Compute-platform-coherence regime, the §3.1 placement of Q-Compute in the IR regime fails. P-Cascade-Continuity is violated at the platform-coherence transition.

Refutation of F1–F5 falsifies the SCBU-projection extension without refuting Papers_053–060 individually (the Q-Compute arc remains internally intact; only the SCBU-IR-projection-extension fails).
