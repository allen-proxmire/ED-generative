epistemic move as Newton's laws or Einstein's SR postulates. All downstream
physics is CONDITIONAL structural derivation given the primitives. Nothing

retired in 2026-05 after external review called it circular).

Key vocabulary:

- **form-FORCED vs value-INHERITED**: ED claims to force the STRUCTURE/FORM of
  a law; numerical constants are usually inherited from measurement, not
  derived. Verdict tiers M1 (fully forced) / M2 / M3 (mostly inherited)
  encode how much of a result is form vs value.
- **crank-safety**: every paper opens with a "What This Paper Does NOT Claim"
  preamble and a step-by-step audit tagging each move P(ostulate) /
  D(erived) / I(nherited) / measured / structural / open. No overclaiming.
- **Wall 1 vs Wall 2**: two different kinds of ED limit. Wall 1 = a
  coarse-graining limit (attainable in principle, mostly open). Wall 2 =
  a proven finite-memory/uncomputable ceiling (e.g. ED provably cannot
  reach the prime-number escape/parity layer — a real, proven "no").
