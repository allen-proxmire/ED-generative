## CORRECTION 2026-07-02

This theorem's "unconditional" registration does not survive the reconciliation audit run 2026-07-02 (recorded in `event-density/docs/ED_Research_Targets.md`, target #8b, and `foundations/Gleason_Rehabilitation_Attempt.md`). It was downgraded on two independent grounds:

This is exactly the conflation Paper_004 was careful to keep separate, reintroduced here as if closed.

**Current honest status (per the 2026-07-02 rehabilitation attempt):** the inner-product / channel-orthogonality foundation is **not** FORCED from the canonical primitives. Three independent derivation routes for **P-Channel-Orthogonality** were tried — P04 additivity, P07 distinctness, P11 phase-randomization — and **all three fail or apply to the wrong layer**, independently confirming Paper_004's own diagnosis. A candidate regime-bound (the sublinear-composition `c₁₂` from `theory/visibility_to_bandwidth.md`) was tried and **withdrawn the same session**: `c₁₂` is interferometric *visibility*, large (not small) for thin-participation systems, and probably not even the same object as `⟨K|L⟩`. Treat T11 (and the Bell–Tsirelson `|S| ≤ 2√2` it carries) as resting on an **open conjecture** (P-Channel-Orthogonality), not an unconditional theorem. See also [T10](T10.md), which was conditional on this result.
