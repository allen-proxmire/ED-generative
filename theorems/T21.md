## §3 Derivation

### 3.1 ECR supplies the composition rule (D-row)

By I-030, substrate-gravity composition is the geometric mean $a = \sqrt{a_N \cdot a_0}$, where $a_N = GM_b/r^2$ is the Newtonian acceleration and $a_0$ is the substrate-invariant MOND scale (T20).

### 3.2 Deep-MOND limit (D-row)

In the deep-MOND regime ($a_N \ll a_0$), the ECR reduces to:

$$a = \sqrt{\frac{G M_b a_0}{r^2}} = \frac{\sqrt{G M_b a_0}}{r} .$$

### 3.3 Circular-orbit equilibrium (D-row)

For a test particle on a circular orbit at radius $r$ with rotation velocity $v$, centripetal-acceleration equilibrium requires $v^2/r = a$. Substituting:

$$\frac{v^2}{r} = \frac{\sqrt{G M_b a_0}}{r} ,$$

which simplifies to:

$$v^2 = \sqrt{G M_b a_0} .$$

### 3.4 Slope-4 form (D-row)

Squaring both sides:

$$v^4 = G M_b a_0 .$$

The slope-4 power $v^4 \propto M_b$ is FORCED by this algebraic composition.

### 3.5 Form FORCED, normalization INHERITED (D-row + I-row)

The functional form $v^4 \propto M_b$ is FORM-FORCED by T19 + T20 + ECR + deep-MOND substitution. The proportionality constant $G \cdot a_0$ is VALUE-INHERITED via T19 (numerical $G$ inherited) + T20 (numerical $a_0$ inherited).
